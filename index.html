Welcome to Tombolos

Welcome to Tombolos, an interactive map for exploring tombolos (sand formations connecting islands to the mainland) and their vulnerability to sea level rise.

Dataset: 233 Tombolos | Geographic Scope: All of Greece

This interactive map is built upon the tombolos database published in the academic research paper by Karkani et al. (2024) in the Journal of Marine Science and Engineering

How to cite:

Maroukian, H., Spyrou, E., Tsiatoura, S., Tzouxanioti, M., & Evelpidou, N. (2024). Sea Level Rise and the Future of Tombolos: The Case of Greece Journal of Marine Science and Engineering, 12(9). https://doi.org/10.3390/jmse12091578
